CH06 target specs declare `1100 x 620`. This candidate grammar uses that as the CH06 working canvas and flags protocol `1280` full-column reconciliation as a pre-Layer-3 Design Authority decision if required.

## Print Readiness

- Entry zones, stop pools, user stop pins, and defended branches must remain legible at book size.
- The normal pullback and sweep zone must not collapse into the stop pool.
- Professional alternatives must stay readable as decision objects, not footnotes.
- No required mark may depend on thin lines alone.
