# Acceptance Rejection Comparison Failure Examples

These examples block weak CH06 comparison plates.

## F1. Two Unrelated Films

What it looks like: each outcome panel has its own setup, scale, and initial path.

Why it fails: the class teaches same start, different evidence, different read.

Detection: hide panel titles. If the shared origin is not obvious, fail the plate.

Fix or stop: restore a shared setup band and shared scale.

## F2. Burst As Proof

What it looks like: a fast break or covering impulse is labeled demand, trap, or continuation immediately.

Why it fails: CH06 requires post-event evidence.

Detection: labels appear on the impulse rather than on the first pullback or reentry.

Fix or stop: move classification to the deciding evidence.

## F3. Covering Drawn As Demand

What it looks like: a vertical forced-covering path uses demand labels, value build marks, or committed-buyer language.

Why it fails: covering closes shorts. It creates no new holders to defend pullbacks.

Detection: the covering-only branch lacks exhaustion or failed pullback evidence.

Fix or stop: show loss of reclaim, no bid rebuild, and exhaustion.

## F4. Missing No-Trade Branch

What it looks like: mixed evidence is forced into covering-only or demand-confirmed.

Why it fails: the target spec requires no-trade for incomplete or conflicting evidence.

Detection: there is no neutral unresolved state.

Fix or stop: add the decision strip. If it cannot fit, request composition review.

## F5. Hidden Scale Change

What it looks like: the defended move looks smaller or larger because the y-scale silently changes.

Why it fails: comparison integrity depends on shared scale.

Detection: identical price distances draw as different visual distances.

Fix or stop: use one shared axis or explicit scale marker.

## F6. Rail-Led Comparison

What it looks like: labels in the rail explain the difference but the panels look similar.

Why it fails: the body must teach the distinction before rails are read.

Detection: cover the rail. If defended, trapped, covering, and demand are no longer separable, fail.

Fix or stop: strengthen outcome marks in the body.
